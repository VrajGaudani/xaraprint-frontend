Cancellation & Refund Policy

Understanding our policy for order cancellations and refunds.

1. Order Cancellation

Due to the custom nature of our print products, orders can only be cancelled within a very limited timeframe after placement, typically before the design approval or before production begins. If you wish to cancel an order, please contact our support team immediately at support@xaraprint.com.

Once an order has entered the production phase, it cannot be cancelled, and no refund will be issued.

2. Refund Policy

As stated in our Terms and Conditions, all custom print orders are non-refundable. Refunds are generally not provided for custom-made products unless there is a manufacturing defect or an error on our part.

**Eligible Cases for Refund/Replacement:**

  • **Damaged or Incorrect Items:** If you receive items that are damaged during transit or are incorrect (e.g., wrong product, wrong design not matching approved proof), you must report this within **48 hours of delivery** with proper photo/video proof. Upon verification, we will offer a replacement or a suitable resolution.
  • **Manufacturing Defects:** If a product has a clear manufacturing defect that affects its functionality or appearance, please report it within 48 hours of delivery with evidence. We will assess the issue and provide a replacement or credit as deemed appropriate.

3. Non-Refundable Situations

  • **Change of Mind:** We do not offer refunds or exchanges if you simply change your mind about a custom order.
  • **Artwork Errors:** We are not responsible for errors in the final product that are present in the artwork approved by the customer. Please review your proofs carefully.
  • **Color Variations:** Slight color variations between your screen and the printed product may occur due to different monitor calibrations and printing processes. These are not considered defects.
  • **Late Reporting:** Claims reported after the 48-hour window from delivery will not be eligible for refunds or replacements.

4. How to Request a Refund/Replacement

To request a refund or replacement for an eligible case, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer support team at support@xaraprint.com within 48 hours of receiving your order.
  2. Provide your order number and a detailed description of the issue.
  3. Attach clear photos and/or videos demonstrating the damage or defect.

Our team will review your request and get back to you with a resolution within 2-3 business days.
